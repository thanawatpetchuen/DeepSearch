<p>Se <b>Spearcletelle</b>, ēac hātte <b>Circolƿyrde</b>, (<a href="/wiki/N%C4%ABwenglisc_spr%C7%A3c" title="Nīwenglisc sprǣc">Nīƿenglisce</a>: <i>computer</i>), is searu <a href="/w/index.php?title=Gewiss&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Gewiss (nā gīet gewriten)">geƿisses</a> mid helpe sumes āƿendendlices <a href="/w/index.php?title=R%C4%ABmbebod&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Rīmbebod (nā gīet gewriten)">rīmbebodes</a> geæfnan mæg.</p>
<p>Spearcletellas nimaþ syndriga ansīena. Ǣrran electronisce spearcletellas ƿǣron þǣre micelnesse miceles rūmes, þegdon sƿā micel cræft sƿā sumu hundredu menniscra spearcletella. <sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Tōdæg cunnon circolƿyrdas bēon genōg smale tō sittenne in <a href="/w/index.php?title=D%C3%A6gm%C7%A3l&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Dægmǣl (nā gīet gewriten)">ƿyrstdægmǣle</a> and tō nimenne cræft of <a href="/w/index.php?title=D%C3%A6gm%C7%A3les_cr%C3%A6fthord&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Dægmǣles cræfthord (nā gīet gewriten)">dægmǣles cræfthorde</a>.</p>
<ol class="references">
<li id="cite_note-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><a href="#cite_ref-1">↑</a></span> <span class="reference-text">In 1946, fræt <a href="/w/index.php?title=ENIAC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="ENIAC (nā gīet gewriten)">ENIAC</a> hƿæthƿēgu 174 kW. Ƿiþ hēodæges spærcletellas brȳcþ sum ymbe 400 W; ofer fēoƿer hundredum sīðum lǣs þonne þæt. <a href="/w/index.php?title=Bysen:Ref_harvard&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Bysen:Ref harvard (nā gīet gewriten)">Bysen:Ref harvard</a></span></li>
</ol>
