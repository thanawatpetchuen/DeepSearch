class="word__name--TTbAA" title="Origin and meaning of computer">computer (n.)</h1><section class="word__defination--2q7ZH"><object><p></p><p>1640s, &quot;one who calculates, a reckoner, one whose occupation is to make arithmetical calculations,&quot; agent noun from <a href="/word/compute?ref=etymonline_crossreference" class="crossreference">compute</a> (v.).</p><p></p><p>Meaning &quot;calculating machine&quot; (of any type) is from 1897; in modern use, &quot;programmable digital electronic device for performing mathematical or logical operations,&quot; 1945 under this name (the thing itself was described by 1937 in a theoretical sense as <span class="foreign">Turing machine</span>). <a href="/word/ENIAC?ref=etymonline_crossreference" class="crossreference">ENIAC</a> (1946) usually is considered the first.</p><p></p><p><span class="foreign">Computer literacy</span> is recorded from 1970; an attempt to establish <span class="foreign">computerate</span> (adjective, on model of <span class="foreign">literate</span>) in this sense in the early 1980s didn&#x27;t catch on. <span class="foreign">Computerese</span> &quot;the jargon of programmers&quot; is from 1960, as are <span class="foreign">computerize</span> and <span class="foreign">computerization</span>.</p><p></p><p></p><ins class="adsbygoogle mobile" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px;" data-ad-slot="4000736339" data-ad-client="ca-pub-7093273770345366"></ins><blockquote>WASHINGTON (AP) -- A New York Congressman says the use of computers to record personal data on individuals, such as their credit background, &quot;is just frightening to me.&quot; [news article, March 17, 1968]</blockquote><p></p><p>Earlier words for &quot;one who calculates&quot; include <span class="foreign">computator</span> (c. 1600), from Latin <span class="foreign">computator</span>; <span class="foreign">computist</span> (late 14c.) &quot;one skilled in calendrical or chronological reckoning.&quot;</p><p></p><p></p></object></section></div></div><div class="related--32LCi"><h3 class="h3_title" title="words related to
